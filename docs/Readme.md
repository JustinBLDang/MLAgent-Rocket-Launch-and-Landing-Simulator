# Unity ML-Agent rocket launch and landing simulator.

Trains rockets to launch and landing. Rockets rewarded on safety of landing and accuracy of path. Color indicators on platform indicate whther rocket is performing well or bad based on reward system allowing real time evaluation of ML Agent's growth. Parallel training alongside usage of GAIL and Behavoiral cloning to reduce training times from 4 hours+ to around 30 minutes for complex behavior.
